Klitka u Witka

If you want a taste of our local history and aren't necessarily a fan of thin crust, Klitka u Witka is the place to go. It is the first pizzeria in our town, founded in 1983. Its rectangular, thick pizza with tomato sauce, grated cheese (who heard of mozzarella fior di late in the mid-80s?) and mushrooms brings back memories. You can order just one slice or take a whole tray of 9 slices to share with your friends. Today the menu also includes Italian-style pizzas, toasted sandwiches and salads.
