Ratuszowa Restaurant

The heart of Nowy Sącz is the Town Hall. An eclectic building built between 1895 and 1897, it houses local authorities and officials. Between council meetings, it is here that couples take their marriage vows of fidelity and honesty. In the basement of the building is the Ratuszowa Restaurant - one of the few places from the previous era that has survived and is still in operation. If you are in Nowy Sącz on a business trip or sightseeing, it is worth stopping at Ratuszowa and ordering the famous pierogi.
