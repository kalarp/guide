Sądecka Winery

In one of the cellars in the town square , a group of friends and wine lovers decided to open a place where they could share their passion with others. There is a wide selection of wines and whiskies to choose from. The owners decided to complement the drinks menu with a range of dishes. You can also take part in some interesting events, such as tasting and live concerts.
