Sushi Zon

Lovers of Japanese cuisine will also find something to their liking. One place in particular stands out - Sushi Zon, near the Jagiellońska paved promenade. It is run by a certified sushi master, a member of the World Sushi Skills Institute. The menu also includes ramen soup, salmon tartare, mochi ice cream and Japanese wine.
