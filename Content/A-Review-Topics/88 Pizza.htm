88 Pizza

Visit 88 Pizza in spring or summer. This is when you can experience the real atmosphere of the place. Eat outside in front of the renovated old-fashioned building, sitting on deckchairs and soaking up the sun. There is something for everyone on the menu. There is Italian pizza, delicious soups, main courses such as cod fillet in wine and butter sauce, tagliatelle in wine sauce with duck and nuts or Thai prawn soup with coconut milk.
