Mr' Jerry Restaurant and Steak House

One of the tastiest places on the culinary map of Nowy Sącz, with an interior reminiscent of popular American steakhouses. The menu is also inspired by American and Italian cuisine. You will be impressed by the range of cold seasoned steaks and burgers made from Hereford beef, pasta or white and red wines. The owners say that even if you don't fancy this type of cuisine, you should try it at Mr' Jerry!
