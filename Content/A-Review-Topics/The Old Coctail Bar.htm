The Old "Coctail" Bar

The history of this misspelled place dates back to 1964. The original taste of desserts, ice creams and iced coffees is probably known to every resident of Nowy Sącz. Today it has a new owner, but the recipes and interior design have remained unchanged. An obligatory stop on any visit to Nowy Sącz.
