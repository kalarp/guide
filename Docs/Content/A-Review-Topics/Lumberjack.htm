Lumberjack Burgers

One of the places I would recommend you visit during your stay in Nowy Sącz is Lumberjacks Burgers. What started as a simple food truck has turned into a restaurant a place that sells meals prepared and served on the premises. with great potential for expansion. At first glance it looks like a typical fast food joint, but the taste of the meat goes far beyond that. You can choose from a menu or 'craft your own Jack' and create your own favourite burger.

Lumberjacks Website

Craft your own Jack!

To personalise your order, follow a few simple steps. You can order from a Lumberjack kiosk or over the counter.

  1. Select a Craft your Jack option from the kiosk menu or ask for it at the counter.

  2. Choose the type of bun: plain or toasted with butter.

  3. Choose the type of burger: single, double or fused.

  4. Add cheese or bacon (why not both?).

  5. Add your favourite vegetables, sauce and condiments.

And voila!
